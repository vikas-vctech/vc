Business Research & Strategy

Industry Research, Market Intelligence & Strategic Consulting

Data is everywhere today. Developing relevant and transferable knowledge about the industry, competitors, and customers is one of the biggest challenges organisations face. We provide a complete array of business research services to tackle such challenges.

Our consultants bring deep domain expertise, applying proven consulting frameworks to produce actionable insights backed by on-the-ground intelligence and extensive primary research.

Market Entry Strategy

Macro-economic environment, industry structure, regulatory complexity and competitive landscape analysis. We focus on manifesting industry potential, gaps and weaknesses, answering: Who are the customers? What is the pricing structure? What is the expected cost structure?

Opportunity Assessment & GTM Strategy

Deep-dive industry analysis to identify potential and risks, screen and filter companies matching client criteria. Go-to-Market strategy covering pricing, distribution channels, and competitive positioning.

Industry Research

Customized industry research across regions and sectors. Interpreting and reconciling conflicting information across sources, providing in-depth analysis using PESTEL and other widely accepted frameworks. All reports tailored to customer requirements.

Feasibility Studies

Detail-oriented feasibility analysis of business ventures or expansion plans. Market analysis, demand analysis, financial analysis, and strategy analysis. Growth plan preparation with recommendations and risk identification.

Competitive Intelligence

Continuous and sporadic support tracking competitors — investment activity, product launches, personnel changes, technology adoption, financial results. Benchmarking financials, products, services and technologies. Advanced competition trackers and executive profiles.

Technology Benchmarking & Innovation Tracking

Scanning secondary research sources, academic publications, patent filings and company reports to identify leading technological breakthroughs. Expert interviews to gain insight into innovation and future trends. Identifying strategy, expansion plans and product launches.

Financial Research

Valuation, Financial Modelling & Investor Intelligence

Our financial research services are tailored for investment banks, private equity firms, capital market companies, and corporate strategy teams. We deliver rigorous, data-backed financial analysis that supports critical investment and strategic decisions.

From initiating coverage reports and sector primers to pitch book support and due diligence, our team brings financial discipline and industry depth to every engagement.

Valuation Reports

Comprehensive company and asset valuation using DCF, comparable company analysis, precedent transactions, and other methodologies appropriate to the asset class and sector.

Financial Modelling

Bespoke financial models for businesses, projects, and investment targets. Three-statement models, LBO models, merger models, and scenario analysis with sensitivity tables.

Sector Primers

Comprehensive overview of an industry — market structure, key players, value chain, financials, regulatory landscape, and investment themes — designed for investors and strategy teams.

Initiating Coverage Reports

Detailed equity research reports covering company background, financials, competitive positioning, growth drivers, risks, and valuation — suitable for initiation of coverage by equity research teams.

Pitch Book & Investor Presentations

End-to-end pitch book support for investment banks and PE firms. Investor presentations for start-ups and growth companies seeking capital, covering market opportunity, business model, financials, and investment thesis.

Due Diligence Support

Commercial and financial due diligence support for M&A and PE transactions — market assessment, competitive landscape, customer analysis, management assessment, and risk identification.

Economic Research

Macro Analysis, Country Assessments & Policy Intelligence

We provide in-depth, comprehensive and regular analysis of macro-economic indicators, geo-political issues, latest developments and key economic issues across regions and countries.

Our economic research capabilities support FX and interest rate strategy, regulatory framework assessments, and thematic studies on the economic forces shaping industries.

Country Reports

Analyzing macro and micro-economic environment, economic indicators, and growth prospects of specific countries for investment and market entry decisions.

Policy Analysis & Benchmarking

Assessment of regulatory frameworks, policy benchmarking across jurisdictions, and strategic recommendations aligned with policy directions and government priorities.

Thematic Studies

Deep-dive thematic research on economic issues — digital transformation, green economy, demographic shifts, urbanization, and other macro trends reshaping industries.

FX & Rate Strategy Support

Support for FX and interest rate product strategies, macro forecasting, and advanced economic trackers delivered as weekly or monthly strategic reports and newsletters.
